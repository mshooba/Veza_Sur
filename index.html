PILSNERS

Pale lager with a crisp, refreshing taste that's lightly hopped. This light-colored lager maintains its bright color while undergoing a process called “lagering” during production

PALE ALES

Hop-forward with a malty flavor, a golden to amber color, and moderate strength. Brewed with pale malt and ale yeast, pale ales bridge the gap between dark stouts and light lagers.

STOUTS

A very dark, full-bodied ale with a distinctive malty flavor that is typically brewed with unmalted roasted barley. Stout is a darker, heavily hopped ale that has a creamy head in the glass
